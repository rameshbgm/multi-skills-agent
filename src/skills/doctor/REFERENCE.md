# Doctor - Reference Guide

## Common Conditions & Symptoms

### Headaches

| Type | Characteristics | Common Causes |
|------|-----------------|---------------|
| Tension | Dull, pressing pain, band-like around head | Stress, poor posture, eye strain |
| Migraine | Throbbing, one-sided, light/sound sensitivity | Triggers vary (food, hormones, stress) |
| Cluster | Severe, around one eye, occurs in clusters | Often unknown, possibly circadian related |
| Sinus | Pressure in forehead, cheeks, nasal congestion | Sinus infection, allergies |

**Red Flags (Seek Immediate Care):**

- Sudden, severe "thunderclap" headache
- Headache with fever and stiff neck
- Headache after head injury
- Neurological symptoms (vision changes, weakness)

### Respiratory Conditions

| Condition | Symptoms | Typical Duration |
|-----------|----------|------------------|
| Common Cold | Runny nose, sneezing, mild cough | 7-10 days |
| Flu | Fever, body aches, fatigue, cough | 1-2 weeks |
| Allergies | Sneezing, itchy eyes, clear mucus | Seasonal or ongoing |
| Bronchitis | Persistent cough, mucus production | 2-3 weeks |

---

### Digestive Issues

| Condition | Key Symptoms | Common Triggers |
|-----------|--------------|-----------------|
| Acid Reflux/GERD | Heartburn, regurgitation | Spicy food, caffeine, lying down after eating |
| IBS | Cramping, bloating, altered bowel habits | Stress, certain foods |
| Food Poisoning | Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea | Contaminated food, usually 6-24hrs after eating |
| Constipation | Infrequent bowel movements, straining | Low fiber, dehydration, inactivity |

## Preventive Health Guidelines

### Recommended Screenings (General)

| Screening | Frequency | Starting Age |
|-----------|-----------|--------------|
| Blood Pressure | Every 1-2 years | 18+ |
| Cholesterol | Every 4-6 years | 20+ |
| Diabetes (A1C) | Every 3 years | 45+ or earlier if risk factors |
| Colorectal Cancer | Every 10 years (colonoscopy) | 45+ |
| Mammogram | Every 1-2 years | 40-50+ (guidelines vary) |

## Mental Health Basics

### Signs of Common Conditions

**Anxiety:**

- Excessive worry
- Restlessness
- Difficulty concentrating
- Physical symptoms (rapid heart, sweating)

**Depression:**

- Persistent sadness
- Loss of interest in activities
- Sleep changes
- Fatigue
- Feelings of worthlessness

**When to Seek Help:**

- Symptoms persist >2 weeks
- Interfering with daily life
- Thoughts of self-harm (emergency)

## Emergency Warning Signs (Call 911)

| Condition | Warning Signs |
|-----------|---------------|
| Heart Attack | Chest pain/pressure, arm/jaw pain, shortness of breath |
| Stroke | Face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty (FAST) |
| Severe Allergic Reaction | Difficulty breathing, swelling, rapid pulse |
| Severe Bleeding | Uncontrollable bleeding |
| Difficulty Breathing | Can't catch breath, bluish color |
